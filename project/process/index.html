My paper prototype

Users click the GO and go to the second page, in the second page, users move the viewpoint (some people understand as moving the charactor) by clicking four directions arrows. When the charactor is close engough, users can read clearly the text. And users are able to click the charactor and the paper scroll on the ground. The charactor will lead users to her own story line, and the paper scroll will lead users to the abstract of chapters.
